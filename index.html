How It Works

Our calculator makes it easy to compare banks and find the best savings options in just a few simple steps.

Step 1: Enter Your Details

Input your savings amount, salary details, spending habits, and other financial information that affects bank interest rates.

Step 2: Choose Allocation Method

Select whether you want to allocate your savings to a single bank or distribute across multiple banks for optimization.

Step 3: Compare Results

Review detailed comparison of interest rates, monthly and annual returns, and see which bank or combination offers the best value.
